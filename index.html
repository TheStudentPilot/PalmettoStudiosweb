Engine

Palmetto Studios is powered by ROBLOX

Palmetto Studios creates games on ROBLOX.com. Most people talking poorly of ROBLOX, but in all seriousness, it is great! We aren't being paid to say this, but with ROBLOX you can:

  • Start learning how to program
  • Learn how to create a game
  • Play games (and create them) 100% for free!
  • Earn real life money

ROBLOX uses Lua, which is easy to use and quick to pick up on. ROBLOX doesn't just teach how to code, but it teaches you the complete process of planning, creating, and releasing a game. People say ROBLOX isn't free, you have to pay to do things. You can develop and play games 100% for free!

Earning Money Through Developer Exchange (DevEx)

You need to pay to get this feature, but it costs $20. When you pay $20 you then can fill out your tax forms and cash out. The minimum payout is $250 USD. I wouldn't mind a $230 return for creating a game and setting myself up for an awesome career in the process! ROBLOX offers the possibilty to, essentially, earn over $1,000,000 per year. They do have a cash out limit, but it's around like $200,000 per year. My family doesn't even make that kind of money!
